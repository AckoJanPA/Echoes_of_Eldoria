Dungeon Floors Overview

Explore the different levels of the dungeon, each with its unique set of challenges and rewards.

Level 1

  • Overall Difficulty Aim: Easy
  • Contains Enemies: Goblin, Slime, Giant Rat
  • Contains Items: Health Potion I, Mana Potion

Level 2

  • Overall Difficulty Aim: Moderate
  • Contains Enemies: Goblin, Orc, Skeleton, Slime
  • Contains Items: Health Potion I, Health Potion II, Strength Potion

Level 3

  • Overall Difficulty Aim: Moderate to Challenging
  • Contains Enemies: Orc, Zombie, Spider, Goblin
  • Contains Items: Health Potion II, Defense Potion, Scroll of Fireball

Level 4

  • Overall Difficulty Aim: Challenging
  • Contains Enemies: Wolf, Bandit, Skeleton
  • Contains Items: Health Potion II, Health Potion III, Speed Potion, Gold Coin

Level 5

  • Overall Difficulty Aim: Significantly Challenging
  • Contains Enemies: Ogre, Ghost, Orc, Bat
  • Contains Items: Health Potion III, Mana Potion, Antidote, Scroll of Teleportation

Level 6

  • Overall Difficulty Aim: Very Challenging (First Dragon Encounter!)
  • Contains Enemies: Dragon, Ogre, Zombie
  • Contains Items: Health Potion III, Strength Potion, Defense Potion, Gold Coin

Level 7

  • Overall Difficulty Aim: High Challenge
  • Contains Enemies: Ghost, Bandit, Spider
  • Contains Items: Health Potion III, Mana Potion, Scroll of Fireball, Gold Coin

Level 8

  • Overall Difficulty Aim: High Challenge

Level 9

Level 10

  • Overall Difficulty Aim: Ultimate Challenge
